Moto X3M Unblocked

Moto X3M Unblocked is an exciting time-trial bike racing game developed by MadPuffers that involves performing awesome stunts on your dirt bike as you make your way through the game’s 22 levels. The riskier the stunts you pull off and the quicker you finish a track, the more stars you will gain. These stars can then be used to upgrade your motorbike. There are numerous obstacles and traps scattered throughout each level, so be careful!

How To Play Moto X3M Unblocked ?

  • Up to accelerate
  • Down to break
  • Left/Right to balance
  • Perform flips to reduce time
