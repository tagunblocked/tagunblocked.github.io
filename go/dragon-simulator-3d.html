Dragon Simulator 3D Unblocked

Dragon Simulator 3D Unblocked

Dragon Simulator 3D Unblocked is a simulation game where you can be a dragon. The goal of the game is to complete the missions of dragons across the level and to collect stars. Once you have enough stars your dragon will upgrade to the next level and become stronger and faster. The missions in this game are really fun to play and they really improve your dragon skills. Fly over the mountains and soar down the peaks to get the feeling of actually flying.

How To PlayDragon Simulator 3D Unblocked ?

  • Dragons are not easy to control:
  • Move around - WASD
  • Spit fire - LMB
  • Attack with your tail - RMB
  • Take-off/Land - Q
  • Fly up - Spacebar
  • Fly down - C
  • Speed up - Shift
  • Lock cursor - L
  • Hide interface - H
