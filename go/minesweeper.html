Minesweeper Unblocked

Minesweeper unblocked

Mine Sweeper Unblocked is a board game created by MarketJS. Enjoy a reimagined classic where a mine field is waiting to be cleared. Use your wits and memory to unravel the tiles you see. Try and avoid the one with the mine, or it's game over! The empty tile will unravel a number that shows the proximity of the nearest mine. There are 3 difficulty setting and countless hours to sink in. Go ahead and try to clear the field!

How To Play Minesweeper unblocked ?

Click or tap on any tile to reveal what it's hiding.
