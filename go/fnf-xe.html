FNF vs XE Unblocked

Boyfriend invited Girlfriend on a date, which was spoiled at one point, because several antagonists noticed the king and challenged him to a musical competition. In order for the couple to continue their romantic evening, the guy in the cap needs to urgently deal with all the rivals standing in the way, one of which is the same dog girl Xe with burning red eyes.

To win, Boyfriend needs to perform the songs to the end. You can see your progress on the indicator at the bottom of the screen, which will show which side has the path to victory. With each correct keystroke, you will move the success slider to Boyfriend's side.
